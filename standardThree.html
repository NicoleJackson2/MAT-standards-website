Standard 3 - Assertive Outreach and Anticipatory Care

This standard is about providing support to those who are thought to be high risk due to drug use.

This standard provides care for those who may be at high risk from drug use. If a person is suspected to be high risk a worker from a substance use service will get in contact first to offer support, instead of waiting for them to reach out.

This benefits anyone who may be at risk from a drug related issue.

If you think someone is at high risk and could benefir from this you can contact your local council, or any support workers.

More detailed information about this standard can be found here on the Government website.
